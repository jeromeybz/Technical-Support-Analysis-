## Overview
This project from Onyx Data that provides a data-driven analysis of technical support operations. It evaluates key performance indicators (KPIs), ticket volume trends, agent performance, and customer satisfaction to identify operational efficiencies and areas for improvement.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The support team maintains a high standard for initial engagement but faces bottlenecks in final ticket closure:

- **Total Ticket Volume:** 2,330 tickets  
- **First Response SLA Compliance:** 86.65% 
- **Resolution SLA Compliance:** 66.39%  
- **Average Resolution Time:** 33.25 hours  
- **Average Survey Score:** 3.51 / 5.0  

---

## Analysis Report
The analysis of the technical support reveals a team that is highly efficient at initial
contact but faces challenges in final resolution speed. The First Response SLA at
86.65%, indicating that customers are acknowledged almost immediately. However, the Resolution SLA stands at 66.39%, with an average resolution time of
approximately 33 hours. While this shows that most issues are solved within the
target timeframe, there is a clear opportunity to bring that 33-hour average down
closer to the 24-hour mark to improve overall customer satisfaction.

During the first two quarters, resolution hours remained consistently below the
annual average, suggesting a high level of operational efficiency. However, performance began to dip in the third and fourth quarters, with resolution times
peaking at 39.19 hours in September. Product Setup is the primary driver of
support volume, accounting for 27.04% of all 630 tickets total. When it comes to
tickets by weekdays, It shows that there is low volume by Weekends compared to
weekdays.

Geographically, Germany and Italy represent the highest volume of inquiries. Despite the high ticket counts in these regions, agent survey scores remain high
and consistent across all priority levels. More than 50% of all tickets are created
via Email, which currently holds a longer response and resolution time compared
to Phone or Chat.

To optimize performance, the department should focus on two
areas creating self-service documentation for Product Setups to solve high volume
of easy-to-solve tickets and implementing strategies to shift more users toward
real-time channels like Chat.
